    Our Message

    Beauty of life has to be reunited in society and human life. Should not allow busy lifestyle and so called development to destroy the beauty of life. The unlimited and unmet needs or greed doesn’t allow society to think about our fellow beings and own future generation which ignore sustainable development. A perfect Social Vision is the need of time for a better future in our planet.

    Indian scenario is in need of committed visionaries for a social change. Weaker sections of the community are becoming weaker and weaker everyday where the need for a social transformation is needed for the well-being of the underprivileged. There is a close give and take between the classes of society. Neglecting the poor is as equal to neglecting the self and our generation.

    Serve what others deserving is something that we serve for our sustainable future. Our fellow beings like human beings, animals, plants, and all that is in the universe is yourself from where you are and to where you are. The only GOD which qualifies the perfection of all history, religion and God thoughts is the Universe.

    Love each other is not the love towards the other but the unselfish love towards the self. Social vision is the love towards the other which is the sustainable love towards the self.

    Social vision is a platform where social visionaries can share their thoughts and ideas for a social change and a platform where resources are served in a complete transparent way.

    Art of serving is soul of social life. Let us serve and train others to serve. Let us build a nation with people who care others.

    We welcome you to be part of initiative for a social transformation.
