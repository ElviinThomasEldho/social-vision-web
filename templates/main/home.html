WHO WE ARE

Social Vision Charity Home is an effort to raise the underprivileged sections of the society by giving priority to the women and children. The main Centre situated in the urban part of Najafgarh, the south west border of Delhi, we work for the cause of Humanity. We believe in executing a system of Caring, Sharing and Empowering. We have been featured in various magazines, weeklies, newspapers and radio shows. We were honored by the visit of Most Rev. Vincent Consessavo, Retired Archbishop of Delhi Archdiocese. The recent visit of Mr. Kailash Gahlot, The Transport and Environment Minister of Delhi and Ms. Saumya Sharma IAS, SDM Najafgarh, during the COVID Campaign was a matter of immense honor.
